Data Quality & AI

For AI, "Quality" means "Signal". Homogeneous or sparse data leads to model failure, even if the data is technically "clean".

The "Boiling Frog" Problem

In sliding-window anomaly detection (e.g., motor vibration), slow degradation can be absorbed as the "new normal" if the window is too short. Quality monitoring requires long-horizon trend analysis.

The AI-Readiness Checklist

  • ✓ Volume: Sufficient for the specific task?
  • ✓ Representativeness: Matches real-world distribution?
  • ✓ Label Quality: Consistent and unbiased annotations?
  • ✓ Bias Check: Absence of hidden patterns?
